 Billing Policy

=========================================
PAYMENT METHODS
=========================================

Customers can pay using:

- Credit Card (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- Debit Card
- UPI
- Net Banking
- Wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay)
- EMI (Available on selected products)
- Cash on Delivery (Available for selected locations)

=========================================
PAYMENT FAILED
=========================================

If your payment fails:

1. Check your internet connection.
2. Verify your bank account balance.
3. Ensure your card details are correct.
4. Try another payment method.
5. Wait 10 minutes before trying again.

If the amount has been deducted but the order was not placed:

- The payment is usually reversed within 5–7 business days.
- If not refunded, contact TechMart Billing Support.

=========================================
INVOICE
=========================================

Customers can download invoices by:

1. Logging into their TechMart account.
2. Opening "My Orders".
3. Selecting the order.
4. Clicking "Download Invoice".

=========================================
GST INVOICE
=========================================

GST invoices are available for eligible business purchases.

Customers should provide their GST Number during checkout.

GST details cannot be modified after the order is confirmed.

=========================================

Prices may change during promotional offers.
