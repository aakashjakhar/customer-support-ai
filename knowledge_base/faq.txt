 Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. A password reset link will be sent to your registered email address.

---

Q2. How do I track my order?

1. Login to your TechMart account.
2. Go to "My Orders".
3. Select your order.
4. Click "Track Order".

---

Q3. How long does shipping take?

Q5. How do I cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped.

Go to "My Orders" and click "Cancel Order".

---

Q6. Can I change my delivery address?

Yes.

You can update your delivery address before the order has been shipped.

---

Q7. How do I download my invoice?

Go to "My Orders".

Open your completed order.

Click "Download Invoice".

Safety Instructions

* Keep devices away from water.
* Use only the original charger.
* Do not open the device yourself.
* Install software updates regularly.
* Back up important data before performing a factory reset.
