Shipping Policy

1. Standard Shipping

* Delivery Time: 5–7 business days.
* Available across India.

2. Express Shipping

* Delivery Time: 2 business days.
* Available in selected cities.

3. Order Processing

* Orders are processed within 24 hours after payment confirmation.

4. Shipping Charges

* Free shipping on orders above ₹999.
* A shipping fee of ₹99 applies to orders below ₹999.

5. Order Tracking

* Customers receive a tracking ID via email and SMS once the order is shipped.
* Orders can also be tracked from the "My Orders" section.

6. Delivery Delays

* Deliveries may be delayed due to bad weather, public holidays, or unforeseen logistics issues.
* Customers will be notified if a delay occurs.

7. Incorrect or Damaged Deliveries

* If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, report it within 48 hours of delivery.
* TechMart will arrange a replacement or refund after verification.

8. Delivery Address Changes

* Customers can update the delivery address only before the order has been shipped.

9. Failed Delivery Attempts

* The courier will make up to three delivery attempts.
* If delivery fails after three attempts, the order will be returned to the warehouse.

10. Contact Support

* For shipping-related issues, contact [support@techmart.com](mailto:support@techmart.com) or call 1800-123-4567.
