 Warranty Policy

All electronic products are covered by a one-year manufacturer warranty unless stated otherwise.

The warranty covers:

* Manufacturing defects
* Hardware failures
* Factory faults

The warranty does not cover:

* Physical damage
* Water damage
* Fire damage
* Unauthorized repairs
* Accidental damage

Customers must provide:

* Original invoice
* Order ID
* Product serial number

Warranty claims are processed within 7 business days after product inspection.

Replacement or repair will be decided by the authorized service center.

Accessories such as cables, adapters, and batteries carry a six-month warranty.

Refund Policy

1. Customers can request a refund within 7 days of receiving the product.

2. The product must be unused and returned in its original packaging.

3. Refund requests must include the Order ID and proof of purchase.

4. Refunds are processed within 5 business days after the returned product is inspected.

5. Refunds will be credited to the original payment method.

6. Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the product was damaged or incorrectly delivered.

7. Products damaged due to customer misuse are not eligible for a refund.

8. Digital products, gift cards, and software licenses are non-refundable.

9. Customers can initiate a refund request through the "My Orders" section or by contacting customer support.

10. If a refund request is rejected, the customer will receive an email explaining the reason.
